Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Pulse. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect different types of information, including:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interaction with our website, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is collected and used for the following purposes:

We take security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
